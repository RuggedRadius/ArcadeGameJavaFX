Controls


Move Left: Left Arrow


Move Right: Right Arrow


Shoot: Space

Music


Music Player

The music is controlled using the above buttons on the main form. Pressing play will play the track, if it is not playing already. The stop button will stop the currently playing track. The next and previous buttons work by advancing the currently palying track to the previous/next track respectively.

Highscores


The highscores are automatically recorded after each game run. Simply enter your name to record the score.


Sorting

To sort the highscores, click the corresponding button to the method in which you wish the highscores to be sorted. The options include sorting by:

  • Name
  • Score
  • Date
